Tips for submitting new memories:
  • Don't get too specific. Often, we only need a hint to ignite our memories!
  • Don't be too general either. "Go somewhere to do something" doesn't help anyone, really.
  • Word your memory as a command, as though you're writing a to-do list (e.g. "Pick up Bart", not "To pick up Bart"/"I need to pick up Bart"/etc.).
  • Refrain from using obscene/offensive language. We would like to maintain a safe-for-work environment.
